# Analyzing data usage on your instance

View a summary of storage consumption and analyze data usage on your current instance over time in the Data Management Console.

## Overview of monitoring data usage

-   [Monitor cloud capacity in Subscription Management](../../subscription-management/concept/monitoring-cloud-entitlements.md)

    View a summary of your cloud storage entitlements and track storage capacity across all the instances associated with your account in Subscription Management.

-   [Analyze data usage on your instance](../task/analyze-data-usage.md)

    Monitor the current size of the database and track additional cloud storage consumption. For example, if you purchased shared storage for your instances, you can determine how much storage is consumed from the pool by your current instance and which tables are consuming the most amount of data.
