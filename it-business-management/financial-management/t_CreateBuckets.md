# Create an account bucket

You can create account buckets in the workbench, but you can also create them with the Bucket form.

## Before you begin

Role required: cost\_transparency\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Financial Modeling** &gt; **Cost Models** &gt; **Buckets**.

2.  Click **New**.

3.  Fill out the fields on the form as appropriate \(see table\).

4.  Click **Submit**.
