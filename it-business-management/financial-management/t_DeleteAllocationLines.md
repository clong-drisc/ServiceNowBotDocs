# Delete allocation lines - Legacy

On the **Configuration** tab of the workbench, you can delete the allocation lines for any fiscal period if you no longer need this data, or if you need to make changes to segments or fiscal periods that are already associated with allocation lines.

## Before you begin

Role required: cost\_transparency\_admin or cost\_transparency\_analyst

## Procedure

1.  Open the workbench and go to the **Configuration** tab.

2.  Under **Advanced Actions**, next to **Delete Data For**, select the fiscal period.

3.  Click **Delete**.

4.  Confirm the action.
