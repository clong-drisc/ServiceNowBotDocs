# Create a new qualified demand

The demand manager can create a new qualified demand from the demand workbench list view.

## Before you begin

Role required: it\_demand\_manager

## About this task

Saving the demand adds it to the Demands list and adds a bubble representing the demand in the bubble chart.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Demand** &gt; **Demands** &gt; **Workbench**.

2.  Click **New** at the top of the list view.

3.  Fill in the fields on the Demand form.

    For information about the fields, see [Creating Demands](t_CreatingDemands.md).
