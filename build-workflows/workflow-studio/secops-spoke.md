# Security Operations spoke

Provides Security Operations actions for flow designers to manage Security Incident Response flow templates.

## Security Incident Response flow templates

The Security Incident Response flow templates are created using [Workflow Studio](../../workflow-studio/reference/workflow-studio.md).

**Note:** Each of the flows is triggered when the **Category** in a security incident is set or changed.

|Template|Description|
|--------|-----------|
|[Security Incident Confidential Data Exposure flow template](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=si-confidential-data-exposure-integrationhub-template&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-security-management&ft:locale=en-US)|Perform a series of tasks designed to handle the exposure of sensitive data.|
|[Security Incident Denial of Service flow template](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=si-denial-of-service-integHub-template&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-security-management&ft:locale=en-US)|Perform a series of tasks designed to handle Denial of Service \(DOS\) attacks.|
|[Security Incident Lost Equipment flow template](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=si-lost-equip-integhub-template&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-security-management&ft:locale=en-US)|Perform a series of tasks designed to handle lost equipment.|
|[Security Incident Malicious Software flow template](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=si-malic-sftwr-integhub-template&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-security-management&ft:locale=en-US)|Perform a series of tasks designed to handle malicious software on your network.|
|[Security Incident Phishing flow template](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=si-phishing-integhub-template&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-security-management&ft:locale=en-US)|Perform a series of tasks designed to handle spear phishing emails on your network.|
|[Security Incident Policy Violation flow template](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=si-policy-violation-integhub-template&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-security-management&ft:locale=en-US)|Perform a series of tasks designed to handle security policy violations.|
|[Security Incident Reconnaissance flow template](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=si-recon-integhub-flow-template&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-security-management&ft:locale=en-US)|Perform a series of tasks designed to handle reconnaissance on your network.|
|[Security Incident Rogue Server or Service flow template](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=si-rogue-integhub-flow-template&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-security-management&ft:locale=en-US)|Perform a series of tasks designed to handle activity from rogue servers or services affecting your network.|
|[Security Incident Spam flow template](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=si-spam-integhub-flow-template&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-security-management&ft:locale=en-US)|Perform a series of tasks designed to handle email spam on your network.|
|[Security Incident Unauthorized Access flow template](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=si-unauth-access-integhub-flow-template&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-security-management&ft:locale=en-US)|Perform a series of tasks designed to handle unauthorized access to your network.|
|[Security Incident Web/BBS Defacement flow template](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=si-web-defacement-integhub-flow-template&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-security-management&ft:locale=en-US)|Perform a series of tasks designed to handle vandalism directed against one of your BBS or web sites.|
