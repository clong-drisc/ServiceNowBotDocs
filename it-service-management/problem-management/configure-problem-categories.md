# Configure problem categories

Configure problem categories to provide a refined way of monitoring and reporting problems. Add to or remove category and subcategory choices from the list of problem categories or subcategories.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Follow the steps in [Define an option for a choice list](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=t_ViewChoiceListDefinitions&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-platform-administration&ft:locale=en-US) to enter your problem categories.
