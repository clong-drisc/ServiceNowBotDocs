# Setting up Slack for communication

The Slack spoke requires creating a custom app on your Slack account to generate OAuth 2.0 tokens for the Slack spoke.

For more information, refer to [Set up Slack spoke](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=set-up-slack&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-integrate-applications&ft:locale=en-US).
