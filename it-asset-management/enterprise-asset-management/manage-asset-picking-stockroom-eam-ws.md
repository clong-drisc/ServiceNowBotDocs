# Manage picking enterprise assets within your stockroom for Enterprise Asset Management workflows

Enable the Asset pick task explicitly for your stockroom and provide granular location details of the asset to facilitate asset picking within your stockroom.

-   **[Enable the Asset pick task for your stockroom in the Enterprise Asset Workspace](../task/enable-pick-task-for-stockroom-eam.md)**  
Enable the Asset pick task for your stockroom so that the task is automatically added to Standard Enterprise Asset requests, Enterprise Asset Refresh requests, and Sourcing workflow.
-   **[Add aisles and spaces to a stockroom in the Enterprise Asset Workspace](../task/add-aisle-space-stockroom-eam-ws.md)**  
Add details of aisles and spaces in a stockroom to provide granular location details of an asset in that stockroom. The aisle and space details enable you to locate and pick an asset in the stockroom.
-   **[View the open enterprise asset pick tasks for your stockroom](../task/view-open-pick-tasks-eam.md)**  
View the open asset pick tasks for your stockroom to get the details of the tasks that need action.
