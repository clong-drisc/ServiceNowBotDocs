# View log data for an alert

View a chart of the frequency of anomalous log lines and the associated log data on the Log viewer.

## Before you begin

Role required: evt\_mgmt\_operator, or evt\_mgmt\_admin

**Important:** From the Vancouver release onward, the **Operator Workspace** is deprecated and replaced with Service Operations Workspace. For the new procedure, see the corresponding topic in the Service Operations Workspace for ITOM documentation: [View log data for an alert](hla-op-logs-log-viewer-sow.md).
