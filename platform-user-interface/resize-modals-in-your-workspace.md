# Resize modals

Resize modals to view content without scrolling horizontally and vertically.

## Before you begin

Role required: workspace\_user

## Procedure

1.  Open a record in your instance.

2.  Select the search icon \(![Search icon](../image/search-icon-new.png)\) within a field.

3.  In the modal, point the mouse over the edges and corners of the modal.

    A double arrow \(![Double arrow cursor](../image/double-headed-arrow-cursor.png)\) appears.

4.  Use the double arrow to drag the edges and corners of the modal to a custom size.
