# Join and end a conference call from incident communications management

After a conference call is initiated, join the call to participate in the discussion or end the call when the conference is no longer required.

## Before you begin

Role required: notify\_view, ia\_admin or admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Incident Communications Management** &gt; **All**.

2.  Open the relevant incident communication plan.

3.  Click the **Conference** tab.

4.  For the relevant incident communications task, under **Actions**, perform one of the following actions.
