# Post a Live Feed message

You can post a message to open a new feed.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Live Feed** &gt; **Live Feed**.

2.  Click the feed to which you want to post your message, for example My Live Feed, Company Feed, and so on.

3.  Enter a message in the **Share your thoughts** field.

    Use hashtags, if you want.

4.  As needed, click ![Poll icon](../image/Poll-Icon.png), ![Attachment icon](../../../common/image/AttachmentIcon.png), or ![Screenshot icon](../image/ScreenshotIcon.png) to add a poll, attachment, or screenshot image, respectively.

5.  Click **Post**.
