# Change your development experience in ServiceNow Studio

Use the best tool for your app development by switching between development environments using the experience switcher in ServiceNow Studio.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin or delegated\_developer

## About this task

At some point in your app development, you may need to create an app in one development environment \(for example, Creator Studio\) and continue working on it in another \(for example, ServiceNow Studio\). Using the experience switcher, you can easily navigate between Creator Studio, ServiceNow Studio, and ServiceNow IDE.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **App Engine** &gt; **ServiceNow Studio**.

2.  Display the experience switcher by selecting the arrow next to the ServiceNow Studio name.

    ![Open the experience switcher to develop in either Creator Studio, ServiceNow Studio, or the ServiceNow IDE.](../image/sn-studio-experience-switcher.png)

3.  Select the development environment you want to open.

    **Note:** The development experiences that you can select from depend on your permissions. Contact your administrator if you need assistance.


## Result

The new development environment opens and you can begin working. For more information, see [Creator Studio](../../creator-studio/concept/creator-studio-landing.md) and [Developing applications with the ServiceNow IDE](../../servicenow-ide/concept/developing-applications-servicenow-ide.md).
