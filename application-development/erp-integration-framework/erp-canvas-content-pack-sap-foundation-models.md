# Zero Copy Connector for ERP SAP Foundation content pack models

The Zero Copy Connector for ERP SAP Foundation content pack contains models you may need when interacting with an SAP system.

## SAP Foundation content pack models

|Model Name|Short Description|
|----------|-----------------|
|Get Address|Retrieve address details based on address number.|
|Get Business Area|Retrieve list of business areas.|
|Get Company Codes|Retrieve list of company codes.|
|Get Controlling Area|Retrieve list of Controlling Areas.|
|Get Country|Retrieve list of countries.|
|Get Currency|Retrieve list of currency codes.|
|Get Inco Terms|Retrieve list of Inco terms.|
|Get Language|Retrieve list of languages maintained in the ERP system.|
|Get Payment Terms|Retrieve list of all payment terms.|
|Get Plant|Retrieve list of plants.|
|Get Plant Locations|Retrieve list of locations within a plant.|
|Get Purch Group|Retrieve list of purchasing groups, along with communication details.|
|Get Purch. Org.|Retrieve list of purchasing organizations.|
|Get Sales Area|Retrieve list of sales areas. \(Sales Area is a combination of Sales Organization, Distribution Channel, and Division.\)|
|Get Valuation Area|Retrieve list of valuation areas.|
