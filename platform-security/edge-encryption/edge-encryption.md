# Edge Encryption

ServiceNow® Edge Encryption ™ encrypts sensitive data on your company premises before sending it over the Internet to your ServiceNow instance \(encrypted in flight\), where it remains encrypted at rest.

**Important:**

Edge Encryption was placed into end-of-sales status as of November 2024, and is in the end-of-renewal phase as of the Yokohama release.

ServiceNow Platform Encryption is the recommended solution going forward. For more information, see [Edge Encryption End of Renewal](https://support.servicenow.com/kb?id=kb_article_view&sys_kb_id=d41196759368ea54c2513f986cba1076).
