# Manage dashboard categories

Dashboard categories enable users to filter the contents of the Dashboard library on terms or phrases assigned by the owner of the dashboard or the analytics\_categories\_admin.

## Before you begin

Role required: analytics\_categories\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Platform Analytics Administration** &gt; **Usage and Governance** &gt; **Analytics Categories**.

2.  Select one or more categories.

3.  From the **Actions on selected rows** menu, choose what to do.
