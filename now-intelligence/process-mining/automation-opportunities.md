# Automation opportunities page

Use the automation opportunities page to find automation opportunities using machine learning and process-based analysis.

![Automation opportunities page](../image/auto-opp-dash.png "Automation opportunities page")

## Summary cards

The cards displayed at the top of the page give you a quick overview of the discovered opportunities. The overview displays how many records are affected, and an estimate of the time you can save by using these automation opportunities.

-   **Automation Opportunities**

    The **Automation Opportunities** card displays a count of discovered by automation discovery. See the discovered opportunities in the **Automation Opportunities** list below this card section.

-   **Possible Deflections**

    The **Possible Deflections** card displays a count of the records covered by the automation opportunities discovered. This count is also visible in the **Automation Opportunities** list below this card section.

-   **Opportunity Volume**

    The **Opportunity Volume** card gives the total records in the top 10 automation opportunities.


## Automation opportunities list

![Automation opportunities list](../image/auto-opp-list.png)

Use the **Automation opportunities** list to see an overview of your opportunities, the estimated mean time to resolve, and what actions you can take to implement these automations.

**Note:** Automation taxonomy is only available for ITSM.
