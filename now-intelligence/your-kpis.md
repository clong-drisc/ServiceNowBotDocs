# Indicator Library in Platform Analytics

The indicator library lists all the Performance Analytics indicators/KPIs to which you have access.

Open the list of indicators at **Platform Analytics** &gt; **Library** &gt; **Indicators**. The list of indicators includes name, trend, score, change, and target. You can sort indicators in ascending or descending order alphabetically or by one of the numerical columns. To filter the indicators, click the three vertical dots and enter the filter terms in the dialog that opens. The filter is applied to the names and the descriptions of the indicators.

You can see any automated, formula, external, or manual indicator to which your roles grant you access. However, you do not see any benchmark indicators. Real benchmarking data is accessible only through the Compare tab on the classic Analytics Hub. You also can see only indicators that have **Show in library** enabled on their indicator records.

Click the name of an indicator to open its KPI Details.

![Platform Analytics KPI library](../../par-for-workspace/image/analytics-center-kpis-tab.png "Indicators list")

## Indicator filter

-   **Bookmarked** shows only your bookmarked indicators. Bookmark any KPI that you have access to so that you can find it easily here.
-   **Created by me** shows only the indicators that you have created.
-   **All** shows all the indicators that you have access to.
